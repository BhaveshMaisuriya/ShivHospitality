Business Overview

We understand that each property is unique, and we tailor our services to meet the specific needs, goals, and challenges of each individual property in our portfolio.

Mission

At VH, our mission is to cultivate a culture of excellence, collaboration, and continuous improvement. We are committed to fostering an environment where every individual is empowered to excel, collaborate, and contribute to positive change.

Accountability

At VH, it's all about math. We carefully analyze data, provide proactive results, and pride ourselves on active engagement with property teams to foster a culture of profit-driven leaders.

Communication

Open, honest, and ethical communication is the foundation of our success. Transparent and available to both our team and property teams, our leadership, rooted in practical experience, offers proven strategies tailored to each organization's needs. In a 24/7 industry, we believe we're all in this together, never closing the door to collaboration.

Performance

Our goal is to inform and educate our property teams with a detailed understanding of our trajectory and the planned route. With a leadership team dedicated to creating increased asset value and nurturing exceptional teams, VH is built for long-term success in this dynamic industry.

Volunteering

At VH, we believe in the power of giving back to the community. Volunteering is not just a task; it's a shared commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of others. Join us on a rewarding journey where your time and skills can create meaningful change.

Culture

At VH, we understand that the hotel industry is a dynamic mix of skill and intuition. Our leadership doesn't just preach; we live and breathe a culture that encourages creative and capable hotel teams, all focused on gaining that crucial revenue share in competitive markets.

We take pride in our nimble ability to adapt to changes, consistently delivering results that surpass expectations. Our Sales, Marketing, and Revenue plans aren't static documents; they're living, breathing tools that evolve in real time with the market, ensuring our teams are not only accurate at forecasting but also accountable for driving and delivering results.
