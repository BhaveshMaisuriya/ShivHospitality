Welcome to Shiv Hospitality

Who we are

At Shiv Hospitality, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional guest experiences with a blend of comfort, elegance, and traditional Indian warmth. Our portfolio of hotels offers world-class amenities, personalized service, and memorable stays for both leisure and business travelers. With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, we aim to set new benchmarks in the hospitality industry. At the heart of our values lies a deep respect for our guests and a passion for creating lasting impressions.

Collaborating with prominent names like Hilton, Best Western, Sonesta, and Wyndham, we efficiently manage properties across diverse locations. Our adaptable plans for sales, marketing, and revenue pivot with market changes, ensuring swift adaptation. Committed to continuous growth, we actively seek new opportunities, maintaining a legacy of excellence.

Going beyond management, we cultivate strong partnerships with major players in the industry, showcasing our dedication to success in the ever-evolving world of hospitality.
